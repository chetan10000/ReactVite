Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EarnApp TV ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. The app does not require account creation, does not request location access, and does not access your contacts, photos, or files.

Advertising ID

The Android Advertising ID is a unique, user-resettable identifier provided by Google Play services. Our third-party advertising partners may access this ID to serve personalized ads and measure ad performance. You can reset this ID at any time in your device settings.

Automatically Collected Information

Our advertising partners may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • IP address (used for general location, not precise location)
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Ad interactions (views, clicks, completions)

How We Use Information

The information collected by our advertising partners is used solely for:

  • Serving relevant advertisements within the app
  • Measuring and improving ad campaign performance
  • Preventing fraudulent activity
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations

Third-Party Advertising Partners

EarnApp TV integrates several third-party advertising SDKs to display rewarded video ads. Each partner operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Ads — https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Start.io (StartApp) — https://www.start.io/policy/privacy-policy
  • InMobi — https://www.inmobi.com/privacy-policy

These partners may use cookies, SDKs, and other tracking technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy. Our advertising partners process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights and Choices

Consent (EEA and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you will be presented with a consent dialog on your first launch of the app. You have the right to:

  • Accept or decline personalized advertising
  • Change your consent preferences at any time
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data (contact us directly)

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data directly. Our advertising partners retain data in accordance with their own data retention policies, typically for periods necessary to fulfill the purposes described in their privacy policies.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information processed through the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

EarnApp TV is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
